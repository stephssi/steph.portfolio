About Me

I'm a Multimedia Artist

Hi! I’m a 24-year-old aspiring creative currently pursuing a degree in Multimedia Arts at Philippine Christian University – Dasmariñas. With a strong passion for visual storytelling and digital design, I specialize in blending art and technology to create compelling multimedia content. My academic journey has equipped me with skills in graphic design, video editing, animation, and photography I’m constantly exploring new ways to bring ideas to life—whether through a dynamic layout, a short film, or interactive media. Driven by creativity and purpose, I aim to produce work that not only looks good but also tells a story and connects with people. Welcome to my portfolio!
